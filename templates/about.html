Panchajanya Vidyapeetha Welfare Trust (R)

Dr. Ambedkar Institute of Technology was established in the year 1980 by Late Sri. M.H. Jayaprakash Narayan, Ex-MLA and Founder Managing Trustee of PVP Welfare Trust and Secretary of the institute. Sri M.H. Jayaprakash Narayan, an Ex-Syndicate/Senate Member of Bangalore University, was a great visionary and an educationist. He was a strong follower of the principles of Bharatha Ratna Dr. B.R. Ambedkar. He came from a very humble Background and was a member of Legislative Assembly for several terms. He fought relentlessly for the upliftment of the downtrodden people. He believed that education is the main path for the emancipation of the oppressed classes and started a number of educational institutions to cater to all levels of education in Bengaluru.

Management

The TRUST has over 24 institutions with more than 10,000 students. As one of the fastest growing organization in the State of Karnataka, it has been offering quality education in every field of academics from Kindergarten to Post-Graduation and Research programs in science and technology at an affordable cost. The students who have passed out of the institutions of PVPWT in the last three and half decades have excelled in their life, bringing laurels and glory to the nation. Eminent academicians, scholars, leaders, industrialists and others have showered accolades on its achievements and accomplishments. The Trust comprises of eminent Trustees with diversified background, who serve relentlessly for the overall development of the organization. The organization is very proud to have these strong visionaries as Trustees.

Anti-Ragging Committee

The institute has a strict anti-ragging policy to ensure a safe and supportive environment for students. The committee:

  • Monitors and prevents any instances of ragging on campus
  • Provides support and counseling for students
  • Ensures strict disciplinary actions against offenders
  • Promotes awareness programs regarding anti-ragging policies

Anti-Ragging Squad

The Anti-Ragging Squad works alongside the Anti-Ragging Committee to ensure a safe and secure environment on campus. Their responsibilities include:

  • Conducting surprise inspections in hostels, classrooms, and campus areas.
  • Monitoring and identifying any incidents of ragging.
  • Taking immediate action and reporting cases to the Anti-Ragging Committee.
  • Spreading awareness about the consequences of ragging through campaigns.

WSW GR Cell

The Women's Safety & Welfare Grievance Redressal Cell (WSW GR Cell) is dedicated to ensuring a safe and secure environment for female students. The cell:

  • Handles complaints related to women's safety and grievances.
  • Organizes awareness programs on women's rights and self-defense.
  • Provides counseling and support for affected students.
  • Works in collaboration with security and administration for a safer campus.
