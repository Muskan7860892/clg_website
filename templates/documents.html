Required Documents for Admission

⚠️ Note: All the documents should be original and one set xerox with BEO signature. ⚠️

Ensure you have the following documents ready:

  • 📜 High school diploma or equivalent certificate.
  • 🆔 Government-issued ID (Aadhar, Passport, etc.).
  • 📄 Passport-size photographs.
  • 📃 Previous academic transcripts.
  • 📑 SSLC Markscard.
  • 📜 Study Certificate (Along with BEO Signature).
  • 📜 Transfer Certificate (TC).
  • 📜 Caste and Income Certificate (Along with BEO Signature).
  • 🏅 Any other Special Category Quota Certificate ( Karnataka Rural, Hyderabad-Karnataka with physically special quota along with BEO Signature).
